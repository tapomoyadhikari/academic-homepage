The first name shall be pronounced as Tapo-moy and last name as Ah-dhih-kah-ree.

So, here's the meaning of Tapomoy

"Tapomoy" is a Bengali word that derives from the Sanskrit root "tapas," which refers to austerity, penance, or a disciplined practice aimed at spiritual growth or self-realization. In a broader sense, "Tapomoy" embodies the essence of asceticism, dedication, and profound contemplation in pursuit of higher ideals or enlightenment. It signifies a state or person imbued with deep spiritual discipline, austerity, and a committed pursuit of inner transformation.
