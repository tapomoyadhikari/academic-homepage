Tapomoy Adhikari

Tapomoy Adhikari is an Indian Computer Researcher currently affiliated with Corgnit Research Lab in Bengaluru. His research interests encompass a broad spectrum of fields, including Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer Networks, and Natural Language Processing.

Adhikari played a pivotal role in founding Byte Research Lab (formerly Byte Technologies R&D Lab), which is dedicated to research in computer networks and computer hardware. Additionally, he is associated with Dycrome, an educational technology company committed to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds, fostering their academic and co-curricular development.

Furthermore, Adhikari is linked with Finessefleet Corporation (India), an educational and technological institution headquartered in Bengaluru. He was instrumental in establishing the Finessefleet Research, which focuses on addressing real-world complex issues through technological advancements.
