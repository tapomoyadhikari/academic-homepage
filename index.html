Tapomoy Adhikari

Tapomoy Adhikari is an Indian Computer Science researcher, serves as Principal Researcher at Corgnit Research Lab in Bengaluru. His expertise spans Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Internet of Things, and network systems.

He has established the Artificial Intelligence Lab at Corgnit Research India which focuses on advanced research on Artificial Intelligence and it also focuses on recent technological trends, Generative AI, AGI, NLP and others.
